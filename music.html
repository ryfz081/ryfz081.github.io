Lunar Gala 2025: Immensity

Credits

Music composed by Ryan Zhang for Carnegie Mellon’s Lunar Gala 2025.

Fashion Designers: Erika Egliskis, Sunny Guo
Description: The designers were aiming for an East Asian ocean theme, and I sought to give the viewer an underwater experience to match the stunning aquatic-themed outfits. I used a combination of orchestral instrumentation and synths to set the scene.
